Italian Pizza

Description

This is the most classic pizza, and the reason we call it classics, because they never run out of style

This recipe will take 40 minutes to make, including the dough

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Dry Yeast
  • Topping
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Mozarella

    • Steps

    • Dough
      1. Mix flour, salt and dry yeast
      2. Add water and blend until dough like
      3. Pour some olive oil over the dough, and let it sit for 20 minutes
    • Topping
      1. Mash tomatoes and boil it down. Add a clove of garlic if you want
      2. Cook down the mixture until its more of a sauce consistency
    • Thin out the dough and add sauce, mozarella bits and a few leaves of basil
    • Put in oven at 230 degreees for about 10 minutes
