Vegetarian Lasagna

Description

Mouth watering, healthy, everyday meal. If you make this you will be the surprise chef to all of your guests(even your children!).

This Vegetarian Lasagna takes 2 hour to finish, but it mainly makes itself.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna plates
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Chili

Steps

  1. Put oven to 220 degrees celcius.
  2. Chop all the greens except the spinach into small cubes
  3. Put in a pan with oil, and let it simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Wash and cook the spinach, add a little salt and pepper
  5. Make bechamel. Here is a link for how to make bechamel
  6. Add greens, spinach, feta, bechamel, lasagna plates in an ovenproof dish, layerwise in told order.
  7. Cover the dish with cheese, and put in oven for 40 minutes.
