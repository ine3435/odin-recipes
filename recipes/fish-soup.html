Fish Soup

Description of dish

This fish soup is originally from Norway, and a classic to have on cold weathered days. With its mild taste and creamy consistency its a win for both adults and young ones! And for sides, a good crunchy slice of bread is never wrong with this soup.

Working time is about 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Fish broth
  • Flour
  • Carrots
  • Salmon
  • Purre
  • Chives
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop all the greens and the fish into medium sized bits
  2. Pour broth and greens into a caserole, an let the vegetables cook for about 15 minutes
  3. After the veggies are semi soft, pour in the milk an let it come to a boil
  4. Lower the temperature and add the salmon, let it sit for about 5 minutes
  5. Taste the soup and add preferred salt and pepper seasoning
